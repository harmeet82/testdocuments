Overview:	Principal Software Engineers have developed one or more of the specialisms, which represent the scope of the software engineering capability, progressing from the level 5 - Leading Software Engineering route or external applicant into CGI. Other routes to this role is by transitioning from an alternate career path such as Architecture.
A member at this stage of their career are deemed subject matter experts in their core specialism in Software Engineering, they may have multiple specialms as well as experiencing aspects of other specialisms. They will be proficient in all core aspects of skills and tooling and exemplify behaviours defined as a Principal software engineer.  

Key Activities: The member will lead and control a delivery of sufficient size and complexity that requires the inclusion of a principal software engineer. The member is likely to lead the project delivery approach and solution, supporting and approving estimations, delivery approach in regards to frameworks, tooling and ensuring all levels of compliance. The member will have developed client relationship and be trusted to deliver on complex and sizeable or scaled agile projects. The member will look to evolve delivery through innovations and create new business opportunities based on their technical and account knowledge.  
The member is expected to be fully self-sufficient in their role and be able to support and be responsible for the technical project delivery solution, grow existing and new opportunities and be aware of their SBU and BU business plans. They are also likely to act as mentors to members in their community who are progressing the same skills, tooling and behaviours.
Members are expected to develop behaviours to the required measured levels before progressing their career level. It is possible for this level to progress to level 7 by achieving the measured standard at the new level for a minimum period of 1 year.
